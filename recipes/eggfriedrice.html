Egg fried rice recipe

Description

Egg fried rice is a popular Asian dish made by stir-frying cooked rice with scrambled eggs, vegetables, and often seasoned with soy sauce, garlic, green onions, and sesame oil. It can be enjoyed as a main dish or a side and is often customized with various meats or additional vegetables.

Ingredients

For the rice:

  • Rice: Preferably day-old, cold cooked rice (jasmine or long grain works best)

Eggs:

  • Eggs: 2 to 3 large eggs, beaten

Vegetables:

  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green onions (scallions), sliced
  • 1/2 cup of frozen peas and carrots (or any other preferred vegetables like bell peppers, corn, or beans)

Seasonings:

  • 2 to 3 tablespoons of soy sauce (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil (optional for flavor)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 to 2 garlic cloves, minced (optional)

Oil for frying:

  • 2 to 3 tablespoons of vegetable or canola oil

Optional add-ins:

  • Cooked protein (chicken, shrimp, beef, tofu, etc.)
  • Other vegetables like bell pepper, mushrooms, or bok choy
  • A dash of white pepper or chili flakes for some heat
  • Freshly chopped cilantro or basil for added flavor

Steps

Preparation:

  1. If you don't have day-old rice, spread freshly cooked rice on a baking sheet and let it cool. For best results, refrigerate for a few hours or overnight.
  2. Beat the eggs in a bowl and season with a pinch of salt.
  3. Prepare all vegetables and keep them nearby for quick access.

Cook the Eggs:

  1. In a large skillet or wok, heat about 1 tablespoon of oil over medium heat.
  2. Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet. Allow them to set slightly and then scramble them. Once cooked, remove the eggs from the skillet and set them aside.

Sauté the Vegetables:

  1. Add a bit more oil to the skillet if needed and increase the heat to medium-high.
  2. Add the chopped onions, and if you're using garlic, add it now. Sauté until the onions become translucent.
  3. Add the other vegetables, like the peas and carrots. Stir-fry until they're tender and slightly browned.

Fry the Rice:

  1. Push the vegetables to the side of the skillet or wok, or remove them and set them aside. Add the remaining oil.
  2. Add the rice to the skillet. Spread it out evenly and let it cook untouched for 1-2 minutes to get a bit crispy. Then, stir and break any clumps.
  3. If you removed the vegetables, add them back in now. Mix everything well.

Season the Dish:

  1. Pour the soy sauce and sesame oil (if using) over the rice. Stir-fry everything to ensure even distribution of the sauces.
  2. Add the scrambled eggs back into the skillet, breaking them into smaller pieces and mixing well.
  3. Season with salt and pepper, adjusting to taste.

Final Touches:

  1. Add any optional proteins or additional ingredients, if you're using them. Mix well.
  2. Add the sliced green onions and stir for another minute.
  3. If desired, sprinkle a dash of white pepper or chili flakes and any fresh herbs like cilantro or basil.

Serve:

  1. Once everything is well-mixed and hot, transfer the egg fried rice to a serving dish or individual plates.
  2. Serve immediately and enjoy!
