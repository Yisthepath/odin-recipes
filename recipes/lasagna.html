Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 lb ground beef (or a combination of ground beef and ground pork)
  • 1 28-ounce can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 15-ounce can tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (optional)
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese (whole milk or part-skim)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12-16 lasagna noodles (regular or no-boil, depending on your preference)
  • 3-4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese (for sprinkling on top)

How to make lasagna

  1. Prepare the meat sauce:
    1. In a large skillet or saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
    2. Add chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
    3. Add minced garlic and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
    4. Add ground beef (or meat combination) to the skillet, breaking it apart with a spoon, and cook until browned.
    5. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, basil, oregano, thyme, salt, pepper, and sugar (if using). Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat and let it cook for at least 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  2. Prepare the cheese mixture:
    1. In a medium bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, grated Parmesan cheese, chopped parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix well.

  3. Cook the lasagna noodles (if using regular noodles):
    1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and lay noodles flat on a clean surface or parchment paper to prevent sticking.

  4. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  5. Assemble the lasagna:
    1. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom.
    2. Arrange a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce (you may need to trim or overlap the noodles to fit).
    3. Spread about 1/3 of the cheese mixture over the noodles, followed by 1/3 of the meat sauce.
    4. Sprinkle 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese over the meat sauce.
    5. Repeat these layers (noodles, cheese mixture, meat sauce, mozzarella) two more times.
    6. Finish with a final layer of noodles, the remaining meat sauce, and a generous layer of shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese.

  6. Bake the lasagna:
    1. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
    2. Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.

  7. Let the lasagna rest for 15-20 minutes before slicing and serving. This allows the layers to set and makes it easier to cut.
